About me

I am a Ph. D. Candidate at University of Toronto. My research interests lie in the interaction of machine learning, security, and privacy. I have worked on multiple problems related to adversarial machine learning, poisoning attacks and defenses, and privacy in deep learning. In my leisure time, I enjoy watching TV shows. I also play basketball and badminton.

I am on the job market for 2023 Fall. If you feel there is a good match, please contact me via my UofT Email. Many thanks for your attention!
